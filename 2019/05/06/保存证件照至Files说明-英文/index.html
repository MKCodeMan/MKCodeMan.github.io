Instructions for saving your ID Photo to Files

2019.05.06 Coder

Foreword

When you have higher quality requirements for your ID photo, and your system is higher than or equal to iOS 11, you can save your photo ID by saving to Files.

Step

1,Click Save to Files

2,Find the ID Photo in the file

If your iOS system is greater than or equal to 11.0, you can find the AppFiles

Click On My iPhone to enter the folder where you found the app.

You will then be able to find all your ID photos saved to the Files, including the ID photo, the print ID Photo, and the first two compression packs.
